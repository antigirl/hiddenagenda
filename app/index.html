ABOUT

A little bit about who we are

HIDDEN AGENDA is an events agency based in Dublin, Ireland. We promote the music we love in the venues we love. You can catch us every Saturday night in the Button Factory as well as one off events around the City.

Hidden Agenda was founded in 2011. We’re a small team who strive to put on the best events possible and combine an artistic element along the way - such as great visuals and artwork. We collaborate with Slipdraft who create unique visual installations at our shows.

Previous artists we’ve worked with include: Darkside & Nicolas Jaar, Daphni, Koreless, Jamie xx, Wolf + Lamb, YACHT, Ghostpoet, Austra, HEALTH, François Kevorkian, Lindstrom, Daniel Avery, Erol Alkan, Dj Koze, John Talabot, Mount Kimbie, Simian Mobile Disco, Mano Le Tough, Machinedrum, Forrests, New Jackson, Ben Pearce, Snakehips, Bondax, The 2 Bears, Soul Clap, Holy Ghost!, Ewan Pearson, Wankelmut, Dj Dez Andrés, Azari & III, Matias Aguayo, Clu, MMOTHS, Cyril Hahn, Mykki Blanco, Todd Terje, Ships, White Collar Boy, Crazy P, Art Department, Benoit & Sergio, Fantastic Mr. Fox, Detroit Swindle, I Am The Cosmos, Moon Boots, Pional, Leon Vynehall, Waze & Odyssey, Maribou State, Aeroplane, Toby Kaar, Actress, Jimmy Edgar, Pillowtalk, Claude VonStroke, Pangaea, Frank B, Krystal Klear, Jacques Greene, oOoOO, Girl Unit, Orquesta, Planningtorock, Subaltern + many more!
